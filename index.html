If God Can Forgive Without Blood, Why a Human Sacrifice?

Isaiah 1:18 shows the Almighty declaring that He Himself can remove guilt and make sinners clean—without slaughtering an innocent man. If forgiveness is available by divine decree, worshipping a created being as “God” becomes unnecessary and, according to Isaiah 43:10, impossible. The Tanakh’s consistent message is repentance, prayer, and obedience—not deification of any future teacher.
